STORIES FUEL SPIRIT

The world revolves around stories. Folklore and fantasy, fairytale and history.
They can excite or amuse us, and they can shock or humble us.
Stories establish communities, whilst teaching and engaging those who listen to them.

Tales ought to be told, and stories ought to be shared.
Let's help bring engaging and entertaining stories to life; to educate, inform
and to brighten our spirits.

OUR WORK

Videography

Videos are incredible tools for sharing stories. They can be used to teach, warn or entertain, and we can help you create media from conception all the way through to post-production.

Recently, we created a promotional video for the RSPCA North Somerset Branch to advertise their big appeal for 2024, using a mix of traditional camerawork, drone photography and motion graphics to create a video that clearly explained their goals.

Graphic Design

Looking for a poster or piece of promotional material designed? We've got you covered. With experience in all forms of graphic design, from digital to print media, we can help you create the eye-catching design you need.

We worked with up-and-coming Audiobook producers, ABP Studios, who were looking for some visually striking cover art for a newly published audiobook in collaboration with the University of Bath.

Brand Design

Creating a recognisable brand is fundamental to establish your work. We can assist you in create a brand package that carefully matches your requirements without compromising your standards, including logo and promotional design, color specifications and tone-of-voice guidelines.

Teaming up once again with ABP Studios, we produced a new branding package for the company, including a new logo, color scheme, set of branding guidelines and a temporary holding website whilst the company made the switch to the new branding.

Character & Story Creation

Characters can help drive an emotional connection with an audience, and are crucial to telling an interesting story. We can assist in creating and designing characters to boost your stories and strengthen your brand.

As part of an animated Halloween display we produced, we created two eerie inhabitants, Mori and Tori, to live inside a dilapidated old manor house.

Concept Design

Concept Design is as crucial as a project itself. Concept art is invaluable when showcasing new plans, and can help tie a collection of ideas together. We can lend a hand to help generate fresh ideas for your project, or assist you in bringing a story to life.

We worked to create some general concept art for a potential 'Beach Race' themed go-kart attraction at a local theme park, called 'Shark Bay Raceway', including 3D models and concept art for the attraction, and a collection of new branding.

Merchandise and Product Design

Quality products are the keystone to a successful brand, and a critically undervalued tool for sustaining business. We can work with you to design and market a product line suitably inline with the stories you are looking to tell.

We designed and budgeted for several concept merchandise products (including print media, clothing, promotional products, and toys) for our concept 'Shark Bay Raceway' attraction, and have created branded products and uniforms for a number of small businesses.

Prop and Scenic Design

Storytelling isn't restricted by imaginary, digital or print means. Physical design and construction can be used in a whole variety of ways to help tell imaginative stories. We can help you to design and construct physical props, sets or displays depending on your requirements, whether that be scenic design for a video telling a unique story or through design of a seasonal display.

We created an interactive Halloween display in 2024, incorporating several effects, technologies, and various multimedia and construction techniques, to tell the story of a ghost couple who roam the halls of a dilapidated manor house.
